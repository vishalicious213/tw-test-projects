About Iconic Web Studios

Iconic Web Studios is a web development company with locations in Santa Clarita and Yorba Linda, CA.

We provide websites for small businesses and individuals. Our services include custom design, development, hosting, SEO and website updates.

Philosophy of Design

Our design tenets include mobile-first design, responsive sites that automatically accommodate phones, tablets and larger screens and SEO optimization. Our key service directive is that clients can request text, video or image edits as needed for no additional charge after a site is live.
